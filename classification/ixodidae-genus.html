Ixodidae - Genus

Anal Groove

Is the anal groove antieor (towards mouth parts) or posterior (away from mouth parts) relative to the anus?

An anterior anal groove will likley mean the genus Ixodes.

A posterior anal groove could mean the genus:

  • Haemaphysalis
  • Bothriocroton
  • Amblyomma
  • Rhipicephalis (Boophilus)
